# SAFe Portfolio Vision

## Definition
Describes the future state of a portfolio's value streams and solutions. A "destination postcard from the future."

## Hierarchy
Mission → Corporate Vision → **Portfolio Vision** → Strategic Themes → Portfolio Backlog (Epics)

## Strategic Themes
- Differentiating business objectives connecting portfolio to enterprise strategy
- Best stated in OKR format, plain business language
- Specific enough to guide Epic prioritization, broad enough for multiple Value Streams
- Example: "reduce time-to-market for digital products by 30%" (good) vs. "be more innovative" (bad)

## Characteristics
- Aspirational yet realistic and achievable
- Motivational — aligned with team purposes
- Provides longer-term context that informs near-term decisions
- "People at work are thirsting for context, yearning to know that what they do contributes to a larger whole"

## Communication
Presented during PI Planning events. Uses presentations, workshops, visual aids.
