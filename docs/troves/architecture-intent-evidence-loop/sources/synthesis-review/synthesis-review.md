# Cross-Experiment Synthesis and Review

**Date:** 2026-03-24

## Where Both Experiments Agree

1. **Evidence is the biggest gap.** Both independently identified that swain has significant investment in Intent (artifact system) and early Reconciliation work (drift detection, retros), but almost nothing on structured evidence. Four-leg: 9/10 gravity. Unified: named "Structured Exhaust" as the initiative most likely to unblock everything downstream.

2. **Execution is not a vision.** Four-leg: 4/10 gravity. Unified: made it an initiative. Both agree execution is a handoff problem, not a destination.

3. **The schema problem is shared.** Intent schema and evidence schema must be designed together — can't define what evidence to capture without knowing what intent it'll be compared against.

4. **VISION-004 is inside the loop, not alongside it.** The reconciliation->intent boundary is fundamentally about operator cognitive load.

## Where They Disagree

Four-leg suggested: two new visions + evolve VISION-001 and VISION-005. Unified suggested: one vision + four children. The disagreement is whether reconciliation earns vision status or is the first initiative spanning Intent and Evidence.

## Reviewer's Assessment

The unified structure is stronger for three reasons:
1. The loop is the insight — splitting it obscures the value
2. Boundary artifacts give better work decomposition — "fix the handoff" is more actionable than "build the system"
3. Avoids bureaucracy — one vision with initiatives worked as pain is felt

**Key risk:** unified vision risks being a tautology (restatement of VISION-001). The loop vocabulary and boundary focus are what make it more than rebranding.

**Lightest-touch option:** keep VISION-001 as root, add loop vocabulary as structural reframe, create boundary initiatives directly. Observable Reality could still exist as peer vision for work with genuine independent gravity.

## Open Questions

- Does Observable Reality deserve standalone vision status, or is it an initiative under the unified root?
- Should VISION-004 be explicitly positioned inside the loop?
- Should VISION-005 absorb execution-fidelity concerns?
- What's the migration path for existing children of the current visions?
